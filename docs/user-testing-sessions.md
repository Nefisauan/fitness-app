# User Testing Sessions

**Product:** FitAI
**Sprint:** 3
**Required:** 3 sessions with observation documentation

---

## Testing Protocol

Each session follows this structure:

1. **Introduction (2 min):** Explain this is a test of the product, not of them. Ask them to think aloud.
2. **Task prompts (15–20 min):** Give tasks one at a time. Observe without helping.
3. **Debrief (5 min):** Ask follow-up questions.
4. **Notes (immediately after):** Fill in observations below.

**Tasks to assign:**
- "Sign up and complete your fitness profile."
- "Generate your AI fitness assessment."
- "Find your personalized workout plan and log a workout."
- "Do a weekly check-in and see your progress."
